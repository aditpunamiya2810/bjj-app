# BJJ-Coach

BJJ AI Coach is an automated sports analytics engine that transforms raw Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and submission grappling footage into world-class coaching feedback. Powered by Google's Gemini 3.1 Pro reasoning model, the app goes beyond basic video description to analyze biomechanics, leverage, and positional tactics.

Simply upload a sparring video, provide a brief description of the players, and the AI will generate a highly technical, frame-by-frame breakdown of the match.

Key Features:

🧠 BJJ Physics Engine: Evaluates hip placement, cervical spine alignment, and rotational levers rather than just naming positions.

🎯 Anti-Swap Tracking: Utilizes "Narrative Anchoring" to maintain accurate player identity through complex scrambles, rolls, and sweeps.

📊 Deep Analytics: Calculates objective performance scores, playstyle statistics, and pinpoints exact timestamps for strengths, weaknesses, and missed back-takes.

⚡ Smart Compression: Features an OS-agnostic, automated FFmpeg pipeline to instantly crush massive 4K video files down to API-friendly sizes before uploading.

📄 Instant Deliverables: Automatically compiles the AI's analysis into downloadable PDF coaching reports and CSV interval breakdowns.
